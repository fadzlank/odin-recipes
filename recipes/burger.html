Burger

Description

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe to create your own delicious burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Onion slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Form ground beef or chicken into burger patties, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and add a small amount of cooking oil.
  3. Cook the patties for 3-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking, allowing it to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly in butter until golden brown.
  6. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard on the buns.
  7. Assemble the burger by layering the patty, tomato slices, onion slices, lettuce, and pickles.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
