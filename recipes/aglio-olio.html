Aglio Olio Pasta

Description

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe to create your own aglio olio pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or any long pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (thinly sliced or minced)
  • Red chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Water (for boiling pasta)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and saut√© until golden and fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  4. Stir in red chili flakes and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the drained pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the garlic oil mixture.
  6. If needed, add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce and coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Toss in fresh parsley and mix well.
  9. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan cheese if desired.
