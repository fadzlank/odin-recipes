Lasagna

Description

This is a quick 5 minute recipe to create your own delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Marinara or tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (optional, for ricotta mixture)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté diced onions and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara or tomato sauce, season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, an egg (optional), and Parmesan cheese. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  10. Add a layer of meat sauce on top of the cheese.
  11. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, sauce) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent the cheese from burning) and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil, if desired.
